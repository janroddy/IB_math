Compound Interest

The formula for calculating the future value of an investment with compound interest is:

$FV = PV\left( 1 + \frac{r}{k(100)} \right)^{nk} $ Example 13: Zoran wants to change 200 Singapore dollars (SGD) to euros. The exchange rate is 1SGD = 0.588 euros. The bank charges 2% commission. Calculate how many euros Zoran receives.
