Printing a Geometric Sequence:

The formula for the nth term of a geometric sequence is: $U_{n} = U_{1} r^{n-1}$

  • $U_{1} = U_{1} r^{1-1}$
  • $U_{2} = U_{1} r^{2-1}$
  • $U_{3} = U_{1} r^{3-1}$

Another way to write the formula for the sum to n terms of a geometric sequence is:

$S_{n} = \frac{U_{1}(1-r^{n})}{1-r} $
